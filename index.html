Delightful for iPhone

A delightful and beautiful Trovebox client for iPhone

Simple and Intuitive Navigation and Gestures

Delightful has been designed to let you browse your photos quickly and efficiently. Use simple and familiar gestures like pinch in/out and swipe up/down to navigate around the app. Photos are grouped by date taken.

Save to Favorites

If you have tons of photos like me, it is hard to find that certain photo from a certain moment. When you find it, simply mark it as 'Favorite' so that you can find it quickly later. Saving to Favorites doesn't download the photo.

Location and Camera Data

If your photo has GPS information, Delightful will show you the address where the photo was taken. You can also see the camera data of the photos.
